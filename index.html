About Us
YojanaSaathi ke baare mein — hamare mission, vision aur team ki jankari
🎯 Hamara Mission
YojanaSaathi ek non-commercial information platform hai jiska uddeshya India ke har nagrik tak sarkari yojanaon ki sahi aur updated jankari pahunchana hai — bilkul free mein, simple Hinglish language mein.
Bahut baar log sarkari yojanaon ke baare mein ya toh jaante nahi, ya phir galat jankari ki wajah se fayda nahi utha paate. Hamare blog ka maksad yahi gap bharna hai.
📖 Hum Kya Karte Hain
  • India ki 500+ central aur state government schemes ki jankari research karke publish karte hain
  • Har yojana ke liye eligibility, documents, apply process step by step explain karte hain
  • Schemes mein jo bhi changes hote hain unhe regularly update karte hain
  • Official government websites ke direct links provide karte hain
  • Common questions ke jawab simple language mein dete hain
🌟 Kyun Trust Karein Hume
  • Hum sirf official government sources se jankari lete hain — PIB, Ministry websites, government portals
  • Koi bhi fees ya charges nahi lete — ye bilkul free service hai
  • Kisi bhi scam ya fraud se koi relation nahi — hum sirf information share karte hain
  • Apply karne ke liye hamesha official government website pe redirect karte hain
  • Har article mein date mention hoti hai — aap dekh sakte hain jankari kitni fresh hai
⚠️ Important Notice
YojanaSaathi ek independent information website hai. Hum Indian Government ya kisi bhi government department ke official representative ya affiliate nahi hain. Yahan di gayi jankari educational purpose ke liye hai. Kisi bhi yojana mein apply karne ke liye hamesha official government website use karein aur kisi dalal ko paise na dein.
